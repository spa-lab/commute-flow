Commute-flow was funded by the ESRC's Secondary Data Analysis Programme 2014 - 2016.

The project was led by Richard Kingston supported by Stephen Hincks and Cecilia Wong in the Manchester Urban Institute at The University of Manchester and Brian Webb at Cardiff University.
