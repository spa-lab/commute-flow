commute-flow Help

First start by selecting a place on the map to view outward commuting for the super-groups.

Clicking on the toggle button located at the right top of the web page a collapsible panel will be revealed.

From there you can choose inward commuting, change to map the groups or switch between visualising lines rather than polygons.
